50 Most Inspirational Quotes Of All Time

Published

1 year ago

on

February 21, 2019

By

Damien Thomas

 

Inspirational quotes can have the power to motivate us, lift our spirit and help us to have more belief in ourselves. The following collection of inspirational quotes are from wonderful people from all over the world and throughout history. It is a wonderful mix of quotes from philosophers, motivational speakers, writers and even presidents.

These quotes are not only some of the most inspirational quotes of all time, they are also some of the most famous quotes about life, happiness and success!

Take a few minutes to read these inspirational quotes if you want a positive boost and you want to start feeling highly motivated and totally inspired.

 

50 Most Inspirational Quotes Of All Time

 

1. Our greatest glory is not in never falling, but in rising every time we fall. – Confucius

 

2. Magic is believing in yourself, if you can do that, you can make anything happen. – Johann Wolfgang von Goethe

 

3. All our dreams can come true, if we have the courage to pursue them. – Walt Disney

 

4. The secret of getting ahead is getting started. – Mark Twain

 

5. Don’t wish it were easier. Wish you were better. ― Jim Rohn

 

6. The secret of change is to focus all of your energy, not on fighting the old, but on building the new – Socrates

 

7. Setting goals is the first step into turning the invisible into the visible. – Tony Robbins

 

8. If you don’t like something, change it. If you can’t change it, change your attitude. – Maya Angelou

 

9. Decide upon your major definite purpose in life and then organize all your activities around it. – Brian Tracy

 

10. Becoming strong doesn’t start in the gym. It starts in your head.

 

11. Try not to become a person of success, but rather try to become a person of value. – Albert Einstein

 

12. An investment in knowledge pays the best interest. – Benjamin Franklin

 

13. Everything you’ve ever wanted is on the other side of fear. – George Addair

 

14. If you can dream it, then you can achieve it. You will get all you want in life if you help enough other people get what they want. – Zig Ziglar

 

15. We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit. – Aristotle

 

16. No one can make you feel inferior without your consent. – Eleanor Roosevelt

 

17. Your time is limited, so don’t waste it living someone else’s life. – Steve Jobs

 

18. Failure will never overtake me if my determination to succeed is strong enough. – Og Mandino

 

19. If you cannot do great things, do small things in a great way. – Napoleon Hill

 

20. Believe in yourself. You are braver than you think, more talented than you know, and capable of more than you imagine. ― Roy T. Bennett

 

21. Your mind is a powerful thing. When you fill it with positive thoughts, your life will start to change.

 

22. No man is free who is not master of himself. – Epictetus

 

23. Decide what you want. Believe you can have it. Believe you deserve it and believe it’s possible for you. – Jack Canfield

 

24. Keep your face always toward the sunshine and shadows will fall behind you. – Walt Whitman

 

25. When you know what you want, and want it bad enough, you’ll find a way to get it. – Jim Rohn

 

26. The two most important days in your life are the day you are born and the day you find out why. – Mark Twain

 

27. Be yourself; everyone else is already taken. – Oscar Wilde

 

28. The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams. – Eleanor Roosevelt

 

29. Too many of us are not living our dreams because we are living our fears. – Les Brown

 

30. Go the extra mile, there’s no one on it. – Grant Cardone

 

31. It is never too late to be what you might have been. – George Eliot

 

32. When you want to succeed as bad as you want to breathe, then you’ll be successful. – Eric Thomas

 

33. A bad attitude is like a flat tire, you can’t get very far until you change it.

 

34. The only person you are destined to become is the person you decide to be. –Ralph Waldo Emerson

 

35. When you stop chasing the wrong things, you give the right things a chance to catch you. – Lolly Daskal

 

36. Always do your best. What you plant now, you will harvest later. – Og Mandino

 

37. Go confidently in the direction of your dreams. Live the life you have imagined. – Henry David Thoreau

 

38. We are all here for some special reason. Stop being a prisoner of your past. Become the architect of your future. – Robin Sharma

 

39. Gratitude is not only the greatest of virtues, but the parent of all others. – Cicero

 

40. Things work out best for those who make the best of how things work out. – John Wooden

 

41. I am not a product of my circumstances. I am a product of my decisions. – Stephen Covey

 

42. Success is a personal standard, reaching for the highest that is in us, becoming all that we can be. – Zig Ziglar

 

43. You have to be odd to be number one. – Dr. Seuss

 

44. You’ll never change your life until you change something you do daily. The secret of your success is found in your daily routine. – John C. Maxwell

 

45. Success is walking from failure to failure with no loss of enthusiasm. – Winston Churchill

 

46. Believe you can and you’re halfway there. – Theodore Roosevelt

 

47. Take action, an inch of movement will bring you closer to your goals than a mile of intention. – Steve Maraboli

 

48. I believe that the only courage anybody ever needs is the courage to follow your own dreams. – Oprah Winfrey

 

49. Don’t let what you cannot do interfere with what you can do. – John Wooden

 

50. Whatever the mind can conceive and believe, it can achieve. ― Napoleon Hill

 

I hope that you enjoyed this collection of some of the most inspirational quotes of all time. Please share these quotes with your friends, family and anyone else who will enjoy them.
